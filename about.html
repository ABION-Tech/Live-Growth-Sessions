The Book

This Book Will
Change Your Life

Most people are not lacking information. They are lacking wisdom — the kind that tells you what to do with what you already know.

This book was born from one question: how do we make the wisdom of old relevant to a generation like this? Not as religion. Not as philosophy. As a daily practice that changes how you live, decide, build, love, and lead.

"It's not a book that has all the answers to the questions in life, but it's a book of deep meditation and action — a book that demystifies the application of wisdom and makes it easier to find fulfillment by doing."

Twelve chapters. One per month. Each built around transformational quotes, guided reflection, and honest self-examination. This is not a book to finish. It is a book to live.

Oyewole did not start with the title. He started with the evenings. A family of six, an evening Bible study, a father who kept returning to the book of Proverbs.
