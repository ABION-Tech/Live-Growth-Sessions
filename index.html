Find answers to common questions about Live Growth Sessions and our community.

What exactly are Live Growth Sessions?

Live Growth Sessions is a monthly community gathering where members come together to discuss, apply, and grow through the wisdom of "This Book Will Change Your Life." Each session focuses on one chapter per month, featuring expert insights, group discussions, and practical application exercises. It's designed for individuals committed to intentional personal growth.

What is the cost of membership?

The cost of membership is simply the ownership of a copy of "This Book Will Change Your Life." There are no monthly fees or hidden charges. Once you purchase your book, you gain lifetime access to all Live Growth Sessions and our community. This approach ensures that everyone has equal access to wisdom regardless of financial means.

When and where do sessions take place?

Sessions are held monthly. The exact date, time, and location will be communicated in advance. We offer both virtual attendance via Zoom and occasional in-person gatherings in select cities. All sessions are recorded and shared with members who are unable to attend live. Virtual sessions also include interactive features like breakout rooms and live Q&A.

Do I need to buy the book to join?

Yes, "This Book Will Change Your Life" is required reading for all members. The book serves as the foundation for our discussions and provides the framework for our growth journey. You can purchase the book through our website or from major retailers.

What makes this different from other book clubs?

Live Growth Sessions stands apart through:

• Expert-led sessions by the author and certified facilitators
• Strong emphasis on practical application over discussion
• Built-in accountability system with peer support
• Comprehensive curriculum spanning 12 months of structured growth
• Access to exclusive resources and tools
• Community of high-achievers committed to transformation
